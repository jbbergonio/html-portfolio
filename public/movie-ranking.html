The Best K-Dramas According to Jearmaleen

My top 3 K-Dramas of all-time.

Start-Up

The show follows the lives of young adults who aspire to become successful entrepreneurs in the competitive world of start-ups.

Crash Landing into You

Crash Landing on You is a popular K-drama series that follows the story of a wealthy South Korean heiress.

Twenty Five Twenty One

Set in the aftermath of the 1997 Asian financial crisis, which had a devastating impact on South Korea.
